The problem we're solving

Why this exists

The threat is real and growing

Phishing is the most common way attackers get into an organization's systems or accounts. Small, under-resourced organizations are frequent targets precisely because they're assumed to have the weakest defenses.

Enterprise tools price them out

Commercial email security platforms are built for companies with IT budgets and security teams. A small nonprofit or student org usually has neither — so they're left with whatever protection comes free with their email provider, which often isn't much.
